## Conclusion

The app has a solid foundation. For real-time travel use, focus on:
1. **Reliability** - Offline mode and sync
2. **Speed** - Quick expense entry
3. **Collaboration** - Real-time group updates
4. **Intelligence** - Smart suggestions and automation
5. **Completeness** - All travel-related features in one place

Prioritize features that solve immediate travel pain points: quick expense entry, accurate currency conversion, reliable offline access, and seamless group collaboration.
